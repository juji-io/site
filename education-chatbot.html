Best Practice


To maximize the ROI of an AI Program Assistant, the best chatbot should achieve multiple goals at once:

  • answer questions including complex ones involving multi-turn interactions.
  • elicit basic information from visitors, e.g., prospective students, for future follow up or program advocacy.
  • introduce new program/service and guide visitors to recommended actions (e.g., filling in an application) based on inferred student interests.
  • assemble KPIs for the leadership and chatbot upkeep.
