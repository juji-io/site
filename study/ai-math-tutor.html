Experience an AI Math Tutor

We are conducting a research study that aims at evaluating the effectiveness of an AI math tutor in the form of a chatbot. We are looking for students at 8th or 9th grade to chat with the AI math tutor for about 45 minutes and provide us with their feedback. To thank those who sign up or complete the sessions early, we will offer:

  • $25 eGift card to each of first 25 qualified teachers
  • $10 eGift card to each of the first 100 qualified students (their parents must sign up)
