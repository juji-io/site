Helping online students succeed in their learning journey is a top goal for many educational institutions. However, universities have limited resources of delivering deeply personalized mentoring and guidance to each student. This cognitive AI chatbot is meant to augment mentors and instructors to get to know each student, understanding their motivations and challenges, answering their questions, inferring their unique psychographic characteristics, and using the insights to best help each student succeed at scale.

What You Should Try

  • What's my Big 5 personalityAsk the chatbot after you chat for a bit (e.g., input 100+ words). Although students can use this to discover about themselves, mentors and instructors can view the unique personality of each student (e.g., extroverted and ambitious vs. shy and thoughtful students) on a dashboard and use the insights to better help students.
    • What's my career DNAAsk the chatbot after you chat for a while (e.g., input 100+ words). Although students can use this to discover their own professional interests or learning styles, mentors and instructors can view the insights (e.g., students who prefer social learning vs. hands-on exercises) on a dashboard and use them to better help students.
