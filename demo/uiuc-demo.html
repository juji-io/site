What You Should Try

Check out several examples of AI-powered student advising capabilities for both prospective and existing students.

  • Help me decide on a Business Major. From time to time, we have observed as much as 25% of prospective students' inquiries asking for academic guidance, which cannot be effectively addressed by a simple answer or a website link.

    Pretend to be a prospective student who is interested in undergraduate programs. Select "I'm a prospective student", and then select the option "Help me decide on a Business Major". Follow the flow to provide authentic information about yourself and see what majors Alma will suggest.
  • Help me with my financial situation. Likewise, when an existing student asks for help, s/he has their unique situation. Simply giving a generic answer or pointing them to a website is a very "cold" response. Instead, an AI chatbot should actively listen to them and then provide relevant options and resources to guide their decision making, just like a human counselor would do but perhaps could not do so for every student 24x7.

    Now pretend to be a current student by selection "I'm a current student" and then select "Help me with my financial situation". Then follow the flow to tell Alma that your dad just lost his job and see how Alma would provide options for you to explore.
  • Take an AI interview Every university/college aims at finding prospective students who can best fit with their programs to improve student retention and success. We are working with universities on the use of an AI interviewer to "interview" applicants for better assessing applicant-program fit to aid Admissions decision making. Pretend to be "a prospective student", then select "Take an AI interview".
  • Who is the new dean of Gies This demo chatbot ingested this UIUC Gies webpage. So try to ask the chatbot questions about the content on that website such as "Who is the new dean of Gies" or "what are the majors does Gies offer".
